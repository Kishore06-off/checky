## Slide 7: Grounding System Deep Dive

### **Fact-Checking Pipeline**

#### **Input Validation**
```
LLM Response
    |
    v
Claim Extraction
    |
    v
Source Verification
    |
    v
Fuzzy Matching (80% threshold)
    |
    v
Grounding Score Calculation
```

#### **Validation Rules**
- **Exact Match**: Quote must exist in source
- **Context Verification**: Claim must be supported by surrounding text
- **Metadata Validation**: Page numbers, sections must match
- **Sentence Completeness**: No fragments allowed

#### **Confidence Scoring**
- **High (0.85-1.0)**: Strong evidence, exact matches
- **Medium (0.6-0.84)**: Partial evidence, some matches
- **Low (0.0-0.59)**: Weak evidence, filtered out
